Our Services

You think it, we do it

Welding

Welding and grinding is part of our everyday routine. This can vary from doing "top-chops" to removing rust.

Chassis overhaul

We can do everything to make your ride more comfortable. We create custom four/two link systems, new bodymounts, modernize axles etc.

Body work

Everything that is made out of steel, is our speciality. We modify bodywork on nearly every car before 1970.

Air-ride systems

If you want your car as low as possible, air ride is the way to go. We install air ride specific systems on every chassis.

Engine swaps

Old cars often don't have the power they should have, and this can be solved by modernizing the drivetrain.

Electical wiring

We do everything from fixing electrical issues, to converting an older car to 12v.

About Us

Who am I?

My name is Phil. My passion for cars started at a young age, I was customizing cars before I even had my license. Back then, it wasn't as serious and I just saw it as a hobby.

Good cars get you from A to B. Great cars, well they just get you into trouble.

The reason that I created my own business around customizing old cars, is that I want to see them driving around more and more, and I would like to help achieve that goal.
